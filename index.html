Han Sung Yoo

Chairman's Statement

School bags have evolved into an indispensable item for students, offering immense convenience in carrying the substantial load of books. Lanka Stock (Private) Limited takes pride in being a trusted manufacturer of school bags.

Our journey began modestly in Hunupitiya, Wattala, with a commitment to providing reliable services. Today, we stand as a prominent organization in the bag and cap manufacturing industry, thanks to our dedication to quality.

Our leading position in production is attributed to our high-quality manufacturing processes, utilizing advanced technology. By employing cutting-edge machinery based on Korean technology, we ensure that our customers receive a service they can trust. As we collectively work towards achieving our mission, we express our gratitude for your support.

Thank You!



C.D.Jayasingha

Managing Directress's Statement

As the Managing Directress of Lanka Stock (Private) Limited, I take pride in our journey from a small building in Hunupitiya, Wattala, to a leading organization in Sri Lanka. With factories in Udugampola and Naththandiya, housing over a hundred dedicated workers, our high-quality products resonate with students' aspirations.

Our commitment to excellence is recognized by the Education Ministry, with our organization meeting and exceeding school bag manufacturing standards. We're honored to have received approval and the MASS LANKA PRIME MINISTERS AWARD.

Lanka Stock (Private) Limited extends its reach to schools and Parent Teachers Associations, offering products at concessionary rates. Renowned for supplying bags globally, we're your go-to for customized school bags and caps.
